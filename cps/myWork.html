Research Projects

3D Cell Visualization

A part of a team who developed the first stages of a tool for the visualization of data from a confocal microscope, converting raw images into a 3D visualization. Programming tools used were C# with the help of Unity Game Engine and Visual Studio. Team Members: Dr. Joiner, Lark Bancairen, Cymantha Blackmon, Michaell Casarona and Mary Kate Morales.

Cyberbullying Detection

Artificial Intelligence (AI) implementation to detect and reduce cyber bullying messages transmitted to a receiver. Thsi was done with the use python, google colab and AI based knowledge. . Team Member: Dahana Moz Ruiz, Aysha Gardner, landy Jimemez, Katlin Champion, Alil Hale-Small and the AI4ALL program.
